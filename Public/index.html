My Chess Progress

Thoughts

I have once again started playing chess. When I was younger, I played chess a lot, but as I got older, I was unable to find friends that wanted to sit down over the board and play. I recently created an account on chess.com to see if I still knew what I was doing. I am learning the blitz format, but I must say I am not the greatest at that. My favorite part of chess is studying the board for a little bit before each move. I like to understand the move before doing it, and also, I look to see if I can understand what my opponent is doing. When playing blitz, I often get behind in time, then blunder away a piece to make things more difficult. I have some goals that I have set for this chess hobby. I don't plan on becoming a grandmaster or anything, but I want to get to a point where I feel comfortable playing against people in the 1500 - 1800 rating area.

Goals

(in no specific order)

  • More blitz wins than losses
  • Blitz rating over 700
  • Lower the frequency I lose on time
  • Complete daily tournament (starts 10/1/2019)
  • Complete puzzles everyday for 1 month
  • Once a month play a few games over the board
  • Setup OBS to stream occasionally
  • Build up confidence to stream
